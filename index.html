Freedom Of Speech

This website was created as part of a school initiative where I decided to create a marketing campaign to raise awarness on the infringement of freedom of speech by governments.

The aim of the campaign is to make you aware of the actions you can take to help those oppresed in foreign countries by advertising notable proects aiming to help the people regain their right to speech.
