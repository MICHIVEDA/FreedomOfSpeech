OPEN UP SPEECH

This website was created as part of a school initiative where I decided to create a marketing campaign to raise awarness on the infringement
of freedom of speech online.

The aim of the campaign is to make you aware of the actions you can take to help those oppressed in foreign countries by advertising notable
projects aiming to help the people regain their right to speech.


- Flash Drives For Freedom - Donate USB sticks to help smuggle information into North Korea. LINK

- Electronic Frontier Foundation Blue Ribbon Campaign - A campaign to show support and raise awarness on the right of free speech. LINK
